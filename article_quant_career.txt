POLYMARKET CONNECTION (LMSR):
- Cost function: C(q) = b * ln(sum(exp(q_i/b)))
- Price of outcome i: p_i = exp(q_i/b) / sum(exp(q_j/b))
- This IS the softmax function powering every neural network
- Market maker max loss = b * ln(n)
